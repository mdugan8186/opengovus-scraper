Overview

This dataset includes 150 thousand businesses licensed with City of New York, Department of Consumer Affairs (DCA). NYC Department of Consumer and Worker Protection (DCWP)—formerly the Department of Consumer Affairs (DCA)—licenses more than 59,000 businesses in more than 50 different industries. Each business is registered with license number, legal business name, dba name, phone, location, license expiration date, etc.
